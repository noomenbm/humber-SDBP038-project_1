About me

Physics Researcher and Front-End Developer

I'm Noomen Belmechri, a Physics researcher transitioning into front-end development, combining scientific thinking with creativity and design. My journey started in academia, where I spent years teaching and working with complex concepts, developing a strong attention to detail and a structured approach to problem-solving. As I explored web development, I discovered a new way to express that mindset by building interfaces that people can interact with. I enjoy creating websites that are not only functional but also intuitive and visually appealing. For me, coding is both a technical challenge and a creative process. I'm currently focused on growing as a developer, learning modern tools and frameworks, and building projects that reflect both my analytical background and my passion for design.
